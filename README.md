## Project Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive exploration of digital literacy, online professional identity, and cybersecurity. The project is divided into five core tasks designed to build a secure and professional digital footprint.

## Module Summaries

* **Task 1: Digital Literacy Presentation**
    An infographic highlighting the definition of digital literacy, safe internet practices, and email etiquette.
* **Task 2: Professional Portfolio**
    Setup and optimization of professional social profiles to establish a credible online identity.
* **Task 3: Digital Platforms**
    Documentation and activity proof across various student-centric digital tools.
* **Task 4: Email Etiquette**
    Practical application of formal communication through sample drafts and an etiquette checklist.
* **Task 5: Cybercrime Case Study**
    An analysis of modern cyber threats and a strategic prevention checklist.
